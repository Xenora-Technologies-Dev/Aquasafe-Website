Types of Water Heaters

Storage Water Heaters

Traditional tank-type heaters that store and heat water for immediate use when needed.

  • 30L to 500L capacity
  • Vertical/Horizontal
  • Glass-lined tanks

Instant Water Heaters

Tankless heaters that provide hot water on demand without storing heated water.

  • Compact design
  • Energy saving
  • Unlimited hot water

Solar Water Heaters

Eco-friendly systems that use solar energy to heat water, reducing electricity costs.

  • Flat plate collectors
  • Evacuated tubes
  • Backup heating

Heat Pump Water Heaters

High-efficiency units that use heat pump technology for maximum energy savings.

  • 3x more efficient
  • Low operating cost
  • Eco-friendly

Commercial Water Heaters

High-capacity systems designed for hotels, hospitals, and large commercial facilities.

  • High recovery rate
  • Heavy-duty build
  • Central systems

Accessories & Parts

Complete range of accessories including expansion vessels, mixing valves, and more.

  • Thermostats
  • Safety valves
  • Heating elements
