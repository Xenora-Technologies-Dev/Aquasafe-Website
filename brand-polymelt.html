POLYMELT: 60 Years of Excellence in Piping

POLYMELT Pipe Systems GmbH is a globally recognized leader in innovative PP-R and PP-RCT plastic pipe solutions, delivering uncompromising quality and durability.

With over 60 years of experience in the piping industry, POLYMELT has established itself as a trusted name across more than 20 countries. The company specializes in manufacturing advanced polypropylene random copolymer (PP-R) and polypropylene random copolymer terpolymer (PP-RCT) piping systems that serve potable water, heating, cooling, and industrial applications.

About POLYMELT

The POLYMELT Difference

Innovation & Technology

POLYMELT combines decades of engineering expertise with cutting-edge technology to develop pipes that exceed international standards. The company invests heavily in research and development to create solutions that meet evolving market demands.

Global Presence

Operating across more than 20 countries worldwide, POLYMELT has established a strong distribution network ensuring product availability and technical support in key markets across Europe, Asia, Africa, and beyond.

Quality Assurance

All POLYMELT pipes are manufactured under rigorous quality control systems and certified by internationally recognized organizations including SKZ, DVGW, WRAS, and ICC (NSF/ASTM Standards).

Sustainability

POLYMELT is committed to sustainable manufacturing practices, producing pipes that are durable, energy-efficient, and environmentally responsible. The company's focus on longevity means fewer replacements and reduced environmental impact.

POLYMELT Product Range

A comprehensive portfolio of PP-R and PP-RCT piping systems designed for every application and requirement.

ECOSAN Pipes

POLYMUTAN Pipes
