About POLYMELT

The POLYMELT Difference

Innovation & Technology

POLYMELT combines decades of engineering expertise with cutting-edge technology to develop pipes that exceed international standards. The company invests heavily in research and development to create solutions that meet evolving market demands.

Global Presence

Operating across more than 20 countries worldwide, POLYMELT has established a strong distribution network ensuring product availability and technical support in key markets across Europe, Asia, Africa, and beyond.

Quality Assurance

All POLYMELT pipes are manufactured under rigorous quality control systems and certified by internationally recognized organizations including SKZ, DVGW, WRAS, and ICC (NSF/ASTM Standards).

Sustainability

POLYMELT is committed to sustainable manufacturing practices, producing pipes that are durable, energy-efficient, and environmentally responsible. The company's focus on longevity means fewer replacements and reduced environmental impact.

POLYMELT Product Range

A comprehensive portfolio of PP-R and PP-RCT piping systems designed for every application and requirement.

ECOSAN PP-R & PP-RCT Pipes

POLYMUTAN PP-R Pipes & Fittings

Premium quality PP-R piping systems engineered for drinking water, heating systems, and commercial/industrial applications. POLYMUTAN pipes deliver superior durability and corrosion resistance for demanding installations.

UV Resistant PP-R Pipes & Fittings

Specialized UV-stabilized PP-R pipes engineered for outdoor and exposed applications. Ideal for solar thermal systems, outdoor water distribution, and areas subjected to direct sunlight with excellent UV protection.

Mechanical PP-RCT Pipes

High-performance PP-RCT pipes designed for demanding industrial and mechanical applications. These pipes excel in high-pressure environments, process water systems, and complex industrial installations requiring superior strength.

PP-R Pipes for Potable Water & Sanitary

Specialized piping solutions for safe potable water distribution and sanitary installations. POLYMELT's food-grade certifications and corrosion-resistant properties ensure the highest water quality standards for residential and commercial properties.

PP-R Pipes for HVAC & Industrial

Comprehensive piping systems for HVAC applications, industrial cooling systems, and district heating networks. These pipes combine thermal efficiency with mechanical strength for complex heating and cooling installations.
