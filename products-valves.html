Types of Valves

Gate Valves

Linear motion valves used to start or stop fluid flow, ideal for on/off control applications.

  • Full bore design
  • Low pressure drop
  • Rising/Non-rising stem

Ball Valves

Quarter-turn valves with excellent shut-off capabilities for quick operation.

  • Quick shut-off
  • Tight sealing
  • Full/Reduced port

Check Valves

One-way valves that prevent backflow and protect equipment from reverse flow damage.

  • Swing type
  • Spring loaded
  • Wafer type

Butterfly Valves

Compact valves for flow regulation and isolation in large diameter pipelines.

  • Wafer/Lug type
  • Low torque operation
  • Large sizes available

Globe Valves

Linear motion valves designed for precise flow regulation and throttling applications.

  • Excellent throttling
  • Positive shut-off
  • Y-pattern available

Pressure Relief Valves

Safety valves designed to protect systems from overpressure conditions.

  • Automatic operation
  • Adjustable settings
  • Safety certified
