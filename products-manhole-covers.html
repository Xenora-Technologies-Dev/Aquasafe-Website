Manhole Covers

Durable Access Cover Solutions

Aquasafe Trading supplies heavy-duty manhole covers and frames designed for safe and reliable access to underground utilities and infrastructure.

Our range includes ductile iron, cast iron, and composite manhole covers suitable for various load classes from pedestrian areas to heavy-traffic highways. All products comply with international standards including EN 124 and meet UAE municipality requirements.

Our Range

Types of Manhole Covers

Ductile Iron Covers

High-strength ductile iron manhole covers for heavy traffic areas and highways.

  • Class D400, E600, F900
  • Round & Square shapes
  • Anti-slip surface

Cast Iron Covers

Traditional cast iron covers for general purpose access applications.

  • Class A15, B125, C250
  • Various sizes
  • Durable finish

Composite Covers

Lightweight composite manhole covers with excellent durability and no scrap value.

  • Anti-theft
  • Lightweight
  • Non-conductive

Recessed Covers

Covers designed to be filled with paving material for aesthetic integration.

  • Infill type
  • Block paving compatible
  • Sealed options

Frames & Gratings

Supporting frames and drainage gratings for complete access solutions.

  • Channel gratings
  • Hinged frames
  • Custom sizes

Utility Covers

Specialized covers for telecom, electrical, and water meter access.

  • Telecom boxes
  • Meter boxes
  • Valve chambers
