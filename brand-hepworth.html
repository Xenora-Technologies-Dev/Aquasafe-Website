Hepworth: Middle East's Premier Piping Solutions

Hepworth PME is the leading manufacturer and supplier of quality thermoplastic piping systems to the building & construction, civil engineering and industrial market sectors.

Established in 2003, Hepworth PME operates a management system based on ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 45001. Advanced Drainage Solutions

uPVC Underground Drainage & Soil Pipe System

High-performance un-plasticized Poly Vinyl Chloride (uPVC) drainage solution designed for efficient wastewater and soil discharge in residential and commercial buildings.

The system features pipes manufactured in terracotta colour for easy underground identification, available in 82 mm drainage pipes and sizes from 110 mm to 315 mm, fully compliant with BS EN 1401 standards. Pipes are supplied in standard 6-meter lengths with special lengths available upon request.

Push-fit & Solvent Joints: Flexible jointing options with EPDM rubber ring seals (BS EN 681)
Thermal Expansion Allowance: 10 mm (3/8") accommodation for pipes up to 4 m length
Chemical Resistance: Excellent against acids, alkalis, and oils
Prefabricated Solutions: Custom items available through dedicated technical service
