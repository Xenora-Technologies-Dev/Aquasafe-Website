100+ Years Durability

Hepworth: Fired to Perfection

Hepworth Clay is the UK's premier supplier of vitrified clay and terracotta drainage systems, combining natural materials with precision engineering for unmatched durability.

With a heritage spanning decades, Hepworth specializes in manufacturing premium clay pipes and fittings that deliver exceptional performance for drainage, heating, ventilation, and roofing applications. Every product is kitemarked to EN 295-1 and CE marked, ensuring superior quality and reliability.

About Hepworth

Premium Clay Products Built to Last

Natural Materials

Made from 100% natural clay with zero synthetic additives. Hepworth's vitrified clay is inherently inert, fully recyclable, and produced with minimal environmental impact using recovered heat processes.

Exceptional Durability

Service life exceeding 100 years with crushing strengths up to 72 kN/m. Hepworth clay pipes demonstrate unparalleled longevity and reliability compared to modern alternatives.

Sustainability Leadership

Fully recyclable clay products with minimal manufacturing impact. Use of recycled aggregate bedding reduces costs and environmental footprint, supporting sustainable construction practices.

Chemical Resistance

Highly inert once vitrified, Hepworth clay resists virtually all chemical attack. Ideal for contaminated sites, brownfield locations, and where sewer effluent exposure is a concern.

Quality Assurance

Manufactured under ISO 9001-approved systems with rigorous in-factory testing. All products are Kitemarked EN 295-1, CE marked, and backed by comprehensive testing and guarantees.

Superior Jetting Performance

Lifetime Jetting Guarantee at 7,500 psi/20 gals/min delivers 50% higher operating pressure than WRc requirements, reducing blockages and maintenance needs significantly.

Hepworth Product Range

Comprehensive portfolio of clay drainage systems for above and below-ground applications.

SuperSleve Drainage

Complete foul and surface water systems with pipes, bends, junctions, and gullies for adoptable sewers and commercial sites.

HepLine Land Drainage

Perforated clay pipes for efficient surface water collection in highways, sports grounds, and agricultural applications.

Inspection Chambers

Range from shallow Mini Access Chambers to deep systems designed for safe, accessible maintenance of drainage networks.

Heating & Ventilation

Terracotta flue liners, chimney pots, and ventilation terminals for traditional and modern heating systems.

Roofing Systems

Ridge tiles, air bricks, and roofing components combining technical performance with traditional aesthetics.

Accessories & Fittings

Gratings, cover plates, channels, yard gullies, and specialized fittings designed to integrate seamlessly.

Advanced Drainage Solutions

uPVC Underground Drainage & Soil Pipe System

High-performance un-plasticized Poly Vinyl Chloride (uPVC) drainage solution designed for efficient wastewater and soil discharge in residential and commercial buildings.

The system features pipes manufactured in terracotta colour for easy underground identification, available in 82 mm drainage pipes and sizes from 110 mm to 315 mm, fully compliant with BS EN 1401 standards. Pipes are supplied in standard 6-meter lengths with special lengths available upon request.

Push-fit & Solvent Joints: Flexible jointing options with EPDM rubber ring seals (BS EN 681)
Thermal Expansion Allowance: 10 mm (3/8") accommodation for pipes up to 4 m length
Chemical Resistance: Excellent against acids, alkalis, and oils
Prefabricated Solutions: Custom items available through dedicated technical service
