Founded in 1945 by Poul Due Jensen, Grundfos has spent over 80 years perfecting pump technology. Grundfos Pump Solutions

Comprehensive range of energy-efficient pumping solutions designed for every application and requirement.

Booster Pumps & Systems

Grundfos booster pumps are designed for increasing water pressure in residential, commercial, and industrial applications, ensuring a constant and reliable water supply.

Transfer Pumps & Systems

Grundfos transfer pumps are ideal for moving water from one location to another, such as from a tank to a water treatment system or for general dewatering tasks.

Circulation Pumps & Systems

Used in heating, ventilation, and air-conditioning (HVAC) systems, Grundfos circulation pumps ensure efficient distribution of hot and cold water in buildings.

Submersible Pumps

Designed to be fully submerged in water, Grundfos submersible pumps are used for a variety of applications, including borehole water supply, irrigation, and groundwater extraction.

End Suction Pumps

Versatile and reliable, Grundfos end-suction pumps are used for water supply, industrial pressure boosting, water transfer, HVAC, and irrigation.

Domestic Pumps

Grundfos domestic pumps provide reliable and efficient water supply solutions for homes, including pressure boosting, wastewater removal, and hot water circulation.

Vertical Multi Stage Pumps

Grundfos vertical multistage pumps are compact and efficient, suitable for a wide range of applications from water treatment plants to pressure boosting in high-rise buildings.
