Grundfos is the largest pump manufacturer in the world, based in Denmark, with more than 19,000 employees globally and annual production of over 16 million pump units.

Founded in 1945 by Poul Due Jensen, Grundfos has spent over 80 years perfecting pump technology. About Grundfos

Founded on Excellence, Driven by Innovation

80+ Years of Experience

Founded in 1945 in Denmark, Grundfos has spent over 8 decades perfecting pump technology and water solutions. The company's heritage is built on innovation, quality, and a deep understanding of water management needs.

Global Presence

Operating across 60+ countries with 20,000 dedicated employees, Grundfos has established itself as a truly global company with local expertise and support in every market.

Sustainability Commitment

Grundfos is committed to addressing the world's most pressing sustainability challenges. The company focuses on reducing energy consumption, protecting water resources, and promoting responsible business practices.

Innovation Leadership

Grundfos invests heavily in research and development to create next-generation pump technologies. Recent innovations like ALPHA GO represent breakthroughs in circulation pump efficiency and digital functionality.

Diverse Applications

Grundfos pumps serve multiple sectors including heating, cooling, water supply, wastewater management, industrial processes, and agriculture across residential, commercial, and utility applications.

Quality & Reliability

Every Grundfos pump is engineered and manufactured to the highest standards, backed by comprehensive warranties and supported by a global network of certified service providers.

Grundfos Pump Solutions

Comprehensive range of energy-efficient pumping solutions designed for every application and requirement.

Circulator Pumps (UP)

Advanced circulation pumps for HVAC systems in private homes, office buildings, and hotels. Used for heating, ventilation, and air-conditioning applications.

Submersible Pumps (SP)

High-performance submersible pumps for water supply, irrigation, greenhouses, and municipal applications with exceptional reliability.

Centrifugal Pumps (CR)

Multi-stage centrifugal pumps for water supply, pressure boosting, and industrial applications with superior efficiency.

Booster Pumps & Systems

Complete booster systems for residential and commercial buildings ensuring consistent water pressure throughout the facility.

Wastewater Pumps

Robust wastewater and sewage pumps for municipal, private, and industrial wastewater management applications.

Fire Pumps

Fire protection pumps compliant with FM, NFPA20, LPCB, VdS, and EN12845 standards for fire sprinkler systems worldwide.

Dosing Pumps

Precision dosing pumps and disinfection systems for accurate chemical treatment in water and process applications.

Electric Motors

High-efficiency electric motors for pumps and separate applications with advanced electronic controls.

End Suction Pumps

Versatile end suction pumps for HVAC, water supply, and industrial processes with easy installation and maintenance.
