Vybik: Precision Valve Solutions

Vybik is a leading manufacturer of high-performance valves for industrial, commercial, and building applications.

Specializing in flow control, pressure management, and safety valve solutions, Vybik delivers reliable products engineered for demanding environments.

Vybik Featured Products

Aquasafe is proud to offer a specialized range of Vybik valves, known for their durability and precision engineering. Our selection includes high-performance ball valves and manifold splitters suitable for a variety of demanding applications.

Vybik Ball Valves

Vybik Ball Valves are engineered for reliable on-off control in a wide range of plumbing and industrial systems. Featuring a full-port design to minimize pressure drop and a robust construction for long-lasting service, these valves ensure a tight seal and smooth operation. They are an ideal choice for applications requiring dependable isolation and control.

  • Full-Port Design for Maximum Flow
  • Durable Brass or Stainless Steel Body
  • Quarter-Turn Operation for Quick Shut-Off
  • Suitable for Water, Oil, and Gas

Vybik Manifold Splitter

The Vybik Manifold Splitter is designed to efficiently distribute flow from a single source to multiple outlets. Ideal for complex plumbing, irrigation, and HVAC systems, this manifold provides a compact and organized solution for flow management. Each outlet is equipped with its own control valve, allowing for precise regulation of individual lines.

  • Multi-Outlet Distribution
  • Individual Flow Control for Each Outlet
  • Corrosion-Resistant Materials
  • Reduces Leak Points and Installation Time
