Complete Drainage Solutions

Aquasafe Trading provides comprehensive drainage system solutions designed for efficient wastewater management in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

Our Range

Drainage System Products

UPVC Drainage Pipes

High-quality UPVC pipes for soil, waste, and rainwater drainage systems with excellent chemical resistance.

  • Sizes: 50mm to 200mm
  • UV stabilized
  • Push-fit joints

Drainage Fittings

Complete range of fittings including bends, tees, reducers, and branches for all drainage configurations.

  • All sizes available
  • Solvent weld type
  • Ring seal type

Floor Drains

Stainless steel and plastic floor drains for bathrooms, kitchens, balconies, and industrial floors.

  • Anti-odor design
  • Multiple grate options
  • Easy to clean

Inspection Chambers

Access chambers for drainage maintenance and inspection, available in various sizes and depths.

  • Pre-formed bases
  • Riser sections
  • Sealed covers

Gully Traps

P-trap and S-trap gullies for connecting surface water and waste water to drainage systems.

  • Odor prevention
  • Various inlet sizes
  • Durable construction

Rainwater Systems

Complete rainwater harvesting and drainage solutions including gutters and downpipes.

  • Gutters & downpipes
  • Leaf guards
  • Storage tanks
