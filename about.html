About Our Company

Empowering Projects with Quality Piping Solutions

At Aquasafe, we are committed to providing high-quality PP-R & PP-RCT piping systems designed for water supply, cooling systems, and compressed air applications. With a focus on durability, efficiency, and corrosion resistance, our advanced piping solutions ensure seamless performance and long-term reliability.

We have gained our reputation of providing and supplying products and services to satisfy our client's expectations. Backed by cutting-edge technology and a dedication to excellence, we deliver easy-to-install, leak-proof, and sustainable piping solutions that meet the highest industry standards.
