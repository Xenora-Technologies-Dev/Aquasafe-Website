Types of Water Pumps

Submersible Pumps

Designed for deep well applications, our submersible pumps operate underwater and are ideal for borehole and groundwater extraction.

  • Deep well applications
  • High pressure capability
  • Corrosion resistant

Centrifugal Pumps

Versatile pumps for water transfer applications, suitable for agricultural, industrial, and domestic water supply systems.

  • High flow rates
  • Easy maintenance
  • Multiple sizes available

Booster Pumps

Enhance water pressure in buildings and facilities with our reliable booster pump systems for consistent water delivery.

  • Pressure enhancement
  • Variable speed options
  • Automatic operation

Industrial Pumps

Heavy-duty pumps designed for demanding industrial applications with high capacity and continuous operation capability.

  • Heavy-duty construction
  • High capacity
  • 24/7 operation

Domestic Pumps

Compact and efficient pumps for residential water supply, garden irrigation, and household applications.

  • Quiet operation
  • Compact design
  • Energy efficient

Fire Fighting Pumps

High-pressure pumps designed for fire protection systems, meeting all safety standards and regulations.

  • High pressure output
  • Emergency ready
  • Certified systems
